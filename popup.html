How to Use

  1. Navigate to your health service biomarkers page
  2. Click "Extract My Biomarkers" to get all your historical data
  3. Export your data in CSV, JSON, or copy to clipboard
  4. Use "Advanced Filters" to customize your data export

Advanced Filtering

  • Latest Only: Get most recent result for each biomarker
  • Date Selection: Choose specific test dates to include
  • Category Filtering: Select which biomarker categories to export
  • Smart Export: Main buttons automatically use your filter settings
  • Session Filters: Filter settings reset when popup closes

Usage Warning

This tool accesses your personal data via manual activation. Do not share or distribute results. Use is subject to the website's Terms of Service.

Tips & Features

  • Auto-Navigation: Extension navigates to correct page automatically
  • Multiple Formats: CSV for spreadsheets, JSON for developers
  • Visual Feedback: Button changes to "Applied" when filters are active
  • Extended Notifications: Messages stay visible longer for easier reading

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure you're logged into your health service account
  • Extension only works on supported health service domains
  • Refresh the page if extraction fails
  • Filters reset each session - this is intentional behavior
